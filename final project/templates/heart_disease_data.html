Heart Disease Data Visualization

Age Distribution and Heart Disease

Here are some data collection of age distribution. Most of the samples are aged more 60. From the plot on the right we see that when people are getting older, they have higher chance to get heart disease. Most of the people who suffer from heart disease are the 80 years and older group. People aged 18-24 and 25-29 suffer least from heart disease.

Smoking and Heart Disease

On the left it is the data of people who smoke and on the right is people who do not smoke. The blue one is people with heart is and the orange one is people without. The plot suggests that when someone does not smoke, he or she will have a lower chance to get heart disease because compared with the smoking sample, more non-smokers report without heart disease and less report heart disease.

Gender and Heart Disease

The plot suggests that male is more likely to get heart disease
